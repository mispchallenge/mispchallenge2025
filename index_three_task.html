Introduction

The challenge considers the problem of audio-visual distant multi-microphone conversational wake-up, speech diarization and speaker recognition in everyday home environments. Both audio and video data are collected in a home TV scenario, where several people are chatting while watching TV in the living room, and they can interact with a smart speaker/TV.

MISP Challenge 2022 features three tasks:

  1. Audio-visual speaker diarization (AVSD)
  2. Audio-visual diarization and recognition (AVDR)
  3. Low-complexity audio-visual wake word spotting (LC-AVWWS)

The details can refer to the links of each task.
